About

Sarah Chen is a seasoned technology executive with over 15 years of experience leading digital transformation initiatives in manufacturing and industrial sectors. As CTO of TechCorp, she has spearheaded the company's transition to Industry 4.0 technologies, resulting in 40% operational efficiency improvements across their global manufacturing facilities.

Sarah holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Stanford University and an MBA from Wharton. She is a frequent speaker at industry conferences and has published numerous articles on digital transformation in manufacturing. Her expertise spans IoT implementation, predictive analytics, and smart manufacturing solutions.

Prior to joining TechCorp, Sarah served as VP of Technology at InnovateCorp, where she led the development of next-generation manufacturing software platforms. She is passionate about helping companies leverage technology to drive sustainable growth and operational excellence.
